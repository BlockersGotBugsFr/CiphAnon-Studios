Deledao

Deledao is an AI-powered web filtering and student monitoring platform used in K-12 schools. It can track and filter browsing activity, blur videos, and flag behavior in real-time.

What Deledao does

  • Real-time filtering of text, images, and videos.
  • Blur/mute inappropriate visuals instead of blocking whole sites.
  • Context-aware AI that understands slang and intent.
  • Classroom tools for teachers to watch screens and block distractions.
  • Wellness monitoring to detect cyberbullying or self-harm searches.
